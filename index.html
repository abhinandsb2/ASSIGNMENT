About

Welcome to SeaMonkey Expeditions, Jordan's Premier Wildlife Experience!

At SeaMonkey Expeditions, we are dedicated to showcasing the remarkable wildlife of Jordan. Join us on an unforgettable journey to witness the Arabian Oryx, stunning birds, graceful water buffalos, and thriving marine life. Our passion for nature and commitment to responsible ecotourism ensure an extraordinary adventure you'll cherish forever. Let us guide you through the untouched beauty of Jordan and its diverse ecosystems, while contributing to the conservation efforts that protect these precious species.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is spotting wildlife guaranteed?

What wildlife are we expected to see in Jordan?

You may see desert foxes, Nubian ibex, various bird species, and sometimes Arabian oryx.

When is the best time to visit Jordan to look for wildlife?

Spring and autumn are the best times, with milder weather and more active wildlife.
